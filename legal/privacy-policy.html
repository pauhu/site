2. Purpose of Data Processing

Pauhu AI uses **artificial intelligence (AI)** to process language data. The data we process is primarily used to:

  • Enhance language communication systems
  • Support translation, rewriting, and summarization tasks
  • Maintain privacy standards and ensure GDPR compliance
We do not process data for profiling or automated decision-making that affects individuals' rights.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent — When you provide explicit consent for us to process your data.
  • Contractual necessity — To fulfill the services you request from Pauhu AI (e.g. translation, rewriting).
  • Legal obligation — For compliance with regulatory obligations under the EU AI Act and GDPR.

4. Data Subject Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request confirmation on whether we process your data
  • Request access to your data or copies of the data we process
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Request restriction on the processing of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data in certain cases (e.g., for direct marketing purposes)
  • Request data portability
To exercise these rights, please contact us at team@pauhu.ai.
